I have recently discovered the art of coding

19W was my first exposure to coding. Having been trained all my life to think in ways applicable to Biology and Chemistry, adjusting the way I approached problem solving and thinking was quite the journey...

I've found coding and the tech world to be fascinating

Creating projects over the course of hours and days, breaking down a huge idea into small bits to create, working with passionate people, and being proud of every the final outcome... All things I hoped for in a career. There is always something to learn and work on.

Why Dali?

Though my coding skills were not strong last term, I have come to a point where I feel I can grow in my abilities while also contributing to a team. I am now comfortable using Python and Java as well as utilizing HTML/CSS/JS and bootstrap frameworks. I love that DALI is so centered on the teamwork and genuine enthusiasm. I want to be able to channel my creativity and problem solving mindset into an environment where the people I work with are all passionate, motivated, and diligent in their ideas and projects.
